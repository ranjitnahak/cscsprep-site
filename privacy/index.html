Privacy Policy

1. Who we are

CSCS Prep is an online CSCS exam preparation programme run by Ranjit Nahak, MSc, CSCS. Our website is cscsprep.in.

2. What information we collect

We collect information you provide directly when you fill out a form on our site. This includes your name, email address, phone number, city, current role, certifications, and your responses to preparation-related questions.

We do not collect any information when you use the Question of the Day feature. Your answers are never logged or stored.

3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Follow up on your enrolment enquiry
  • Send you information about upcoming cohorts and CSCS Prep programmes
  • Respond to your questions or requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Emails

If you submit an enrolment enquiry, you will receive a confirmation email from us. You may also receive updates about upcoming cohorts. You can opt out at any time by replying to any email with "Unsubscribe."

5. Cookies

We use Vercel Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymised data such as page views, device type, and country. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

6. Data storage

Your information is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorised access.

7. Your rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, or to ask us to delete it. To make a request, email us at ranjit@cscsprep.in.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, email us at ranjit@cscsprep.in.
